College Event Calendar

The College Event Calendar System is a digital platform designed to streamline event management across the campus. It helps students, faculty, and staff stay informed about upcoming events, workshops, and academic schedules. The system provides a centralized platform to view, add, edit, and manage event details in one place. Users can easily track important dates and plan their participation in advance. It supports real-time updates and notifications to ensure no event is missed. It enhances communication and transparency within the college community. The system encourages active student involvement and better time management. Overall, it ensures smooth coordination and boosts campus-wide engagement.
